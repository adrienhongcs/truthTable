Truth Table Generator

Enter your boolean expression according to the following acceptable characters:
  • Logical operators
    • "&" (AND)
    • "|" (OR)
    • "!" (NOT)
    • "^" (XOR)
    • ">" (IMPLIES)
    • "=" (IF AND ONLY IF)
  • Parenthesis
    • "("
    • ")"
  • Variables
    • Any other character (a variable is at most one character)
  • Example: A | B > !( A & !C)
